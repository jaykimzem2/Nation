How to Use This Calculator

Get a Realistic Picture of Your Monthly Repayment

This calculator uses standard mortgage amortisation to give you an instant estimate of what your monthly repayment could look like based on the property value, your down payment, the loan term, and the current interest rate.

All calculations are in Kenyan Shillings (KES). This is a planning tool — contact our team for a fully personalised and binding quote.

💡 What is a Down Payment?

A down payment is the amount you pay upfront toward the property. For most Kenyan mortgages, this is typically 10–20% of the property value. The remainder is what you borrow.

📊 What Interest Rate Should I Use?

We've pre-filled the average Kenyan mortgage rate. This fluctuates with the Central Bank of Kenya's base rate. Our team can advise you on the current rate for your specific product.

📅 Which Loan Term is Right For Me?

A longer term means lower monthly payments but more total interest paid. A shorter term means higher monthly payments but you own your home outright sooner. Our loan officers will help you find the right balance.
