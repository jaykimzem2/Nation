What is the Home Kit?

The Free Mortgage Home Kit is a resource package designed specifically for first-time home buyers in Kenya. It is your starting point and your compass on the journey to owning your first home.

  • 📘 A Comprehensive Guide: Understanding the Kenyan mortgage landscape.
  • ✍️ A Workbook: Plan your finances, track your savings, and prepare your documents.
  • 🗺️ A Roadmap: Step-by-step timelines from application to moving in.
  • 🛡️ Expert Insights: Learn how financing works, what documents are required, and how to make the most of your mortgage.
