The Free Mortgage Home Kit

Your essential toolkit for the journey from "Renting" to "Owning" in Kenya.

What is the Home Kit?

In collaboration with KPRA (Kenya Professional Realtors Association), Nationwide Mortgage provides this comprehensive resource package designed specifically for first-time home buyers in Kenya. It is your starting point and your compass on the journey to owning your first home.

  • 📘
    A Subscription to Knowledge: Understanding the Kenyan mortgage landscape.
  • ✍️
    A Workbook: Plan your finances, track your savings, and prepare your documents.
  • 🗺️
    A Roadmap: Step-by-step timelines from application to moving in.
  • 🛡️
    Expert Insights: Learn how financing works, what documents are required, and how to make the most of your mortgage.

How to get it?

The kit is provided free of charge to first-time home buyers who sign up for a KPRA-endorsed Nationwide Mortgage workshop or a one-on-one consultation.
